B. Data Points
1. CrewAI and LangGraph are examples of frameworks facilitating agent choreography.
2. API access to social platforms requires business verification and consent scopes.

C. Implications
1. Infrastructure must prioritize orchestration reliability, data security, and scalability.
2. A phased implementation approach is recommended to mitigate integration risks.

### 3\. User Experience & Workflow Analysis

A. Key Insights
1. Users need clear decision support — AI should explain why a suggestion was made (explainability).
2. Workflows must align with current agency routines: briefing, planning, review, approval, and reporting.
3. A hybrid human-in-the-loop system is essential for trust in early adoption.

B. Data Points
1. Users prefer dashboards showing predicted vs actual performance metrics.
2. Agencies often use CRM and Google Workspace for campaign planning.

C. Implications
1. UI must include confidence scores, rationale explanations, and easy export of recommendations.
2. UX must integrate with existing tools like Sheets, Slack, CRM.

### Executive Summary (2-3 paragraphs)

The global influencer marketing industry is rapidly growing, projected to exceed tens of billions in annual spend by 2027, driven by social commerce and creator economy trends. However, agencies and brands still predominantly rely on manual decision-making and intuition to plan content, select influencers, and forecast performance. There is a clear market gap for an AI-driven solution that can predict content effectiveness, optimize partner selection, and tie creative recommendations to business outcomes. Bagana AI is uniquely positioned to solve this pain point with multi-agent orchestration tailored to strategic planning, not merely content generation.

Technically, the project is feasible with emerging AI coordination frameworks like CrewAI, vector databases for long-term memory, and integration with platform analytics APIs. However, care must be taken in designing agent interaction protocols and operational tooling. Early market validation suggests that agencies and mid-sized brands are willing to adopt predictive tools that reduce cost per conversion and improve campaign ROI — provided that results are measurable and recommendations are explainable. This research brief outlines the key market insights, technical requirements, UX direction, and operational strategy needed to make Bagana AI both viable and competitive.
